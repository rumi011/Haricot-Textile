Homepage > Our Culture > OUR VALUES

The company’s values were upright dealing, ‘honest value’ and full satisfaction. In the words of it’s founder, to us this reputation is a very precious thing. We treasure it as we treasure our dignity. For this reputation, this dignity, this family pride, gives our customers full confidence that we small never sell anything that in quality and sterling worth might be held to be below that high standard set for us.

We respect the rights, differences and dignity of all members of the Aster organization and the wider community. Through the Aster Foundation, we Endeavour to bring about long-term improvements for people and the environment in which they work. We recognize our broader responsibilities and we are committed to safeguarding the interests of future generations. Projects include investments in educational facilities and community health initiatives.

Aster is passionate about helping to protect the future of the environment, demonstrated through our commitment to planting and regeneration, as well as training programs to raise awareness of our global environmental challenges. Social responsibility is very important to us and underpins the way we conduct ourselves, treat our staff and customers, and engage with the world beyond our company

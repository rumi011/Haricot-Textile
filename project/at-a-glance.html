Haricot Textile becomes a fully operational cloth manufacturing Dhaka, Bangladesh . Haricot Textile is creating the supply chains of the future, with a mission to make life better for people everywhere along our global supply chains.We offer end-to-end supply chain solutions from product design and development, raw material sourcing, factory selection, production management and quality control, to in-country logistics, global freight management and e-logistics.

Our business is built upon our people, our time-honored values, financial strength, our culture of innovation, our approach to sustainability and the social impact we make through the Haricot Textile Foundation. we are dedicated to investing in the future where our reach makes life better for people everywhere.
